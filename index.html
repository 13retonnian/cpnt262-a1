About Halloween

What is Halloween?

Halloween is a spooky holiday where people celebrate the scary things in life
People like to dress up, trick-or-treat, have parties and have a ton of fun!
Halloween is the best holiday of the year!

Where does Halloween come from?

Halloween used to be an old pagan festival that celebrated the Autumnal Equinox.
During this time pagans believed that the spirits of the dead would return to the earth.
It was then changed into All Saints Day when society became Christian.
It then morphed into Hallowed Evening.
Then eventually became Halloween
